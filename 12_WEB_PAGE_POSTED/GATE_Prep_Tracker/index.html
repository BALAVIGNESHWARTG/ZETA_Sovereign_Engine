Master Study Resources

Curated list of the absolute best materials specifically for the GATE DA paper. Do not waste time reading multiple books for one subject; stick to these proven standard resources.

Engineering Mathematics Core

  • 📺

    Linear Algebra: Gilbert Strang (MIT OCW)

    The global gold standard. Watch on YouTube. Focus on Matrices, Eigen vectors, and Vector spaces.

  • 📺

    Probability & Stats: Khan Academy / NPTEL

    Use Khan Academy for intuition, then switch to NPTEL GATE portal for rigorous numerical problem solving.

  • 📖

    Book: Higher Engineering Mathematics (B.S. Grewal)

    Use strictly as a reference for Calculus (Optimization) problems.

Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence

  • 📺

    StatQuest with Josh Starmer (YouTube)

    Perfect for understanding the "why" and "how" behind ML algorithms (PCA, Trees, SVM) before tackling math.

  • 📖

    Book: Introduction to Statistical Learning (ISLR)

    Read the chapters on Regression, Classification, and Unsupervised Learning. Extremely relevant for GATE DA.

  • 📖

    Book: AI - A Modern Approach (Stuart Russell)

    Reference for Search Algorithms and Logic. Only read syllabus-specific sections.

Programming, DSA & Databases

  • 💻

    Python & DSA: GeeksForGeeks / Leetcode (Easy)

    Focus on implementation of Stacks, Queues, Trees, and standard sorting/searching algorithms in Python.

  • 📺

    DBMS: Knowledge Gate (Sanchit Jain) / NPTEL

    Excellent breakdowns of Normalization (1NF to BCNF) and Relational Algebra. High yield topics.

Official Portals & Mock Tests

  • 🌐

    NPTEL GATE Preparation Portal (Official)

    Mandatory. Contains mapped video lectures and official previous year questions (GATE 2024, 2025, 2026).

  • 📝

    Test Series (MadeEasy / GO / Ace Academy)

    Purchase at least one reputed test series by December 2026. Mock tests are non-negotiable for time management.
